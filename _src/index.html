Getting started

PHP School is a set of ever expanding workshops to teach you basic to advanced concepts in PHP. We want to empower you to build, whether it be a hobbyist project or a tool to help educate the world. Skill nor age should be a barrier to learning, the PHP School team are committed to helping train the next generation of coders who will go on to change the world.
